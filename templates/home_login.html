Frequently Asked Questions

How does facial recognition attendance works?

Facial Recognition attendance works by using advanced algorithms to analyze and identify unique facial features of individuals. When a person's face is detected in the uploaded image, the system compares it with the stored facial data to mark attendance.

Can the system accurately identify individuals in various lighting conditions?

Absolutely. Our system incorporates adaptive algorithms that can handle diverse lighting conditions, including low light and harsh sunlight. This adaptability ensures consistent and accurate identification, regardless of environmental lighting variations.

What are the benefits of using face recognition for attendance management?

Using face recognition for attendance management provides several benefits, including improved accuracy, efficiency, and security. It automates attendance recording, reduces administrative workload, minimizes errors, and enhances data integrity.

What happens if someone is not recognized by the system?

In the rare event that someone is not automatically recognized by the system, one can manually mark the attendance for individuals who may face recognition challenges. This ensures that attendance records remain accurate and complete, even in situations where automated recognition may not be achieved.
